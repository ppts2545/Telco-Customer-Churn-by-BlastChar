# Business Recommendations

## 1) Segment-Based Retention Program

- Prioritize high-risk segments by contract type and tenure.
- Launch targeted offers (discount, contract upgrade, bundled services) for top-risk groups.

## 2) Early-Life Customer Protection

- Focus on customers with short tenure where churn risk is typically highest.
- Add onboarding checkpoints in first 30-90 days.

## 3) Pricing and Payment Friction Reduction

- Review high-risk payment channels and billing complaints.
- Offer easier payment methods and proactive billing support.

## 4) Service-Level Action Plan

- Use churn drivers from model explainability to define service improvements.
- Build action matrix: driver -> owner team -> expected KPI impact.

## 5) KPI Tracking

Track monthly:

- Churn rate by segment
- Retention campaign acceptance rate
- Revenue retained from intervention group
- Lift vs control group

## 6) Recommended Operating Rhythm

- Weekly: Risk list refresh and campaign deployment.
- Monthly: KPI review and model monitoring.
- Quarterly: Feature refresh and model retraining.
