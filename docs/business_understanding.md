# Business Understanding

## Problem Statement

The business is facing customer churn that impacts recurring subscription revenue.

## Objective

- Predict churn risk at customer level.
- Identify churn drivers that can be acted on by business teams.
- Support retention strategy prioritization with data evidence.

## Success Criteria

- Model can separate likely churners from non-churners with strong recall and stable precision.
- Business can define retention actions by segment (contract type, tenure, payment method, service usage).
- Insights are translated into measurable KPIs (retention uplift, churn reduction, revenue preserved).

## Scope

- In-scope: Exploratory analysis, data cleaning, feature analysis, model development, evaluation.
- Out-of-scope: Production deployment and real-time scoring pipeline.

## Main Data Signals

- Customer tenure
- Contract type
- Monthly and total charges
- Payment method
- Service subscriptions and usage-related attributes
